Vibrant Velachery (VV)

Conception of group VV was in July 2013, for TWCM 2013 practice with a countable number of running, enthusiasts. TWCM 2013 fever is down, but runners kept coming to MRTS velachery never stopped as it become a habit. This ideally took shape as Vibrant Velachery in Feb 2014.


VV practice schedule

VV’s practice running, cross training with cycling and Triathletes practice swimming in SDAT

Pinned runs – on Tuesday/ Thursday/ Saturday

Assemble: 5:15 a.m. for pre-run stretches and warm-up

Run: 5:30 a.m. sharp

Start/end Location – Infront of MRTS velachery railway station

Wednesday – Recovery day or Cross training with Cycling (Gear/Non Gear, MTB/Hybrid/Roadie – all fine)

Thursday – Flyovers repeat /St.Thomas Mount - hill running, distance 3/5/7k
